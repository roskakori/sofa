Support

All tools and libraries have manuals which mention where to obtain support for them.

General questions about Eiffel can be discussed in the newsgroup comp.lang.eiffel. There are also mailing lists for SmallEiffel and Gobo; refer to the manual for details and how to subscribe.

Comments on Sofa, problems with the installation or Amiga-related usage, corrections and bug-fixes can be submitted to Thomas Aglassinger <agi@sbox.tu-graz.ac.at>.
